TCG Manager — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08

TCG Manager ("the app") is a football trading-card manager game. This page explains what information the app collects, why, and how you can access or delete it.

Information we collect

  • Account information: the email address and password you sign up with, plus a display name, team name, and username you choose in-app.
  • Game data: your card collection, squad, match history, season stats, gems balance, friends, chat messages, trades, and challenges — all needed for the game itself to function.
  • Purchase information: if you buy gems or exclusive cards, the purchase is processed by Stripe (web) or Google Play Billing (Android) — we receive confirmation that a purchase succeeded and which product was bought, never your full card number or payment credentials.

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, or device identifiers, and we do not use any third-party advertising or analytics trackers.

How we use this information

Solely to run the game: authenticating you, saving your progress, enabling friends/trading/chat features, and crediting real-money purchases. We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

Who else can see it

  • Supabase (our database/auth provider) stores all of the above on our behalf.
  • Stripe processes web/iOS purchases; Google Play Billing processes Android purchases. Each only receives what's needed to complete your purchase.
  • Other players can see your display name, team name, and avatar (needed for friends/trading/matchmaking) — never your email or password.

Your data, your choice

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time, either from inside the app (Profile tab → Danger Zone → Delete Account) or from this website without installing the app: Delete my account.

Deletion is immediate and permanent — your profile, cards, gems, match history, friends, messages, trades, and purchase records are all removed and cannot be recovered.

Children's privacy

TCG Manager is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.
